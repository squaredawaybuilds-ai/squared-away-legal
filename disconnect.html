Disconnect the Connector from QuickBooks Online

Disconnecting the Squared Away QuickBooks Connector takes two steps: revoking the OAuth grant in QuickBooks Online, and removing any locally stored tokens and credentials on the operator's device.

Step 1: Revoke authorization in QuickBooks Online

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online at qbo.intuit.com.
  2. Click the gear icon (top right) → Apps.
  3. Find Squared Away QuickBooks Connector under "Connected apps".
  4. Click Disconnect and confirm.

Intuit immediately invalidates the OAuth tokens issued to the app for that company. Any further API calls from the Connector will return an authorization error.

Step 2: Remove locally stored credentials

On the device where the Connector is installed, run:

rm ~/.quickbooks-mcp/tokens.json

This deletes the locally cached refresh token and access token. To fully uninstall the Connector, also delete the installation directory and remove the .env file containing the Intuit Developer Client ID and Client Secret.

Reconnecting later

To reconnect after disconnecting, see Connect.
